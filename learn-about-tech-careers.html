Your Future Might Be in Tech, Here's What That Means

Tech is everywhere, in the apps you use, the music you stream, the transport you take, and even the way food is delivered. But behind all of that are real people solving real problems with creativity, logic, and innovation.

And guess what? You could be one of them.

Tech isn't just for people who've been coding since they were kids. It's for anyone who's curious, willing to learn, and excited about shaping the future. Whether you're into design, numbers, people, or storytelling, there's a place for you in tech.

Let's take a look at some of the different paths you could follow:

Software
Development

Build the tools the world runs on.

Developers create the apps, websites, and tools we use every day, from banking apps and video games to booking systems and chat platforms. It;s about solving problems with code, thinking logically, and turning ideas into reality.

Think about your favourite game, like Call of Duty or Fifa, a team of developers made that experience possible from the ground up.
